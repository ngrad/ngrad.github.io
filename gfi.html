Global Financial Integrity

Art director and project lead, branding, user interface, user experience and responsive design, information architecture, wordpress (front end)

May 2014

Global Financial Integrity is a Washington DC non-profit research and advisory organization producing reports on illicit financial flows, the illegal movements of money from one country to another.

GFI fights everyday for government and financial transparency and needed two things from us: to elevate their website brand and to succinctly describe their mission, making their missions unwaveringly clear. When they came to us for website redesign we rolled up our sleeves up to conduct a nitty gritty card sorting exercise to ensure thoughtful information architecture to wireframes to a polished visual design. Since a main problem was colleagues in their space recognizing who they were, we placed their mission at the optimal reading location on the homepage and used strong imagery of boats, which is typically how money leaves said countries. By expanding their color palette beyond the blues in their logo, their website has clear hierarchy and a fresh new look.
